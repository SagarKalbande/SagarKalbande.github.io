Description

Handling a derailed crane in order to place it back on track is a long procedure. At VizExperts, I had the opportunity to work as the Lead Developer for this POC. Railway Crane Simulator is a multiplayer VR Simulation where players can join collaboratively and operate to place a derailed train bogie back on track. It demonstrates the protocol that is essential for executing the process. The crane can be controlled using a Physical Joystick. This makes the player give a higher level of immersion that makes him feel as if he is operating the crane physically.
